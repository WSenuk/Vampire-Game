HOW TO PLAY

  • You start by facing forward in a dark room.
  • After a moment, you hear a vampire scream.
  • Pay attention to which direction the sound came from (left, right, or front).
  • Use the arrow keys to turn in that direction:
  • ⬅️ Left Arrow = turn left
  • ➡️ Right Arrow = turn right
  • ⬇️ Down Arrow = face front
  • Once you're facing a direction, press the SPACEBAR to shoot.
  • If you're facing the correct direction, you'll kill the vampire and earn a point.
  • If you're wrong, the vampire will scream and kill you.
  • Press ENTER to restart the game after dying.
  • Survive as long as possible and keep track of your kills!
